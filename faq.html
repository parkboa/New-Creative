Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about joining, preparing for sessions, and contributing to the lab.

Getting Started

We run open enrollment each semester. You can drop into any studio night before officially applying to see if the vibe is right for you.

Do I need experience?

No. Pods intentionally mix beginners with mentors. Bring curiosity and we will teach the rest.

What should I bring?

Laptop if you have one, but we keep shared kits—sketchbooks, tablets, sound recorders, and cameras—for anyone to borrow.

Is there a membership fee?

No fees. We ask for at least two volunteer hours per semester to keep the studio open and supplies restocked.

How do teams get formed?

Every sprint starts with interest surveys so we can match members with the mediums, partners, or causes they care about most.

Program Logistics

Planning a field trip or workshop? Here’s what to expect.

Attendance

RSVPs help us plan materials, but walk-ins are always welcome for internal sessions.

Field Trips

Transportation stipends are available; request one at least five days in advance.

Accessibility

We provide captioned slides, quiet rooms, and sensory kits—email us if you need something specific.

Project Credits

Submit a short reflection and documentation after each sprint to earn transcript credit.
